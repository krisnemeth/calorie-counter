Having problems using the app?

Check out our step-by-step guide below:

On your Home Page tap the Food Groups button. Pick a category, and you'll be taken to a page, where you can start selecting items you have consumed that day.

On the Food Items page, you will find a table of contents with some related information displayed. Scroll through the items, tap the 'Add' button next to an item, and it will add it to your daily calorie count.

Useful tip: If you want, you can just type in the name of the item you're looking for into the search bar.

After you've added every item you've consumed during your day, it's time to visit the Counter page. When you get to the page, you'll see the overall amount of calories you've consumed. You can reset the counter with the orange button below. We suggest you do this at the end of your day, so you can keep track of your consumption, and see any differences or similarities that occur.

Useful tip: You can tap the handy little calculator icon on the bottom to take you there, or you can select it from the navigation items.

If you'd like to understand calories better, head over to the About page to learn more.

Do not hesitate to get in touch if you have any further questions. Tap the mail icon on the bottom right, or select Contact from the navigation menu.
